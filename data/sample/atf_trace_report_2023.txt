ATF FIREARMS TRACE SUMMARY REPORT
Case No: ATF-2023-0457
Document Date: March 14, 2023

OVERVIEW
This report summarizes firearms recovered and traced during a trafficking
investigation centered in Chicago, IL. A total of 42 firearms were recovered.
The predominant firearm type was the pistol, with several Glock and Smith &
Wesson models identified. The incident type was classified as trafficking.

ORIGIN OF FIREARMS
Multiple firearms were originally manufactured by Glock and Ruger. A cluster of
weapons was sold by Eagle Point Firearms, a federally licensed dealer in
Gary, IN. Investigators determined that several handguns were purchased by
Marcus Webb through a series of straw purchases.

CONNECTIONS
The same dealer, Eagle Point Firearms, appears in two earlier recoveries in
Milwaukee, WI. Marcus Webb is linked to a separate shooting incident recorded
under Case No: ATF-2022-1199. The Glock pistols recovered share a serial range
consistent with a single shipment.
