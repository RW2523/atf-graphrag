ATF INCIDENT RECORD
Case No: ATF-2022-1199
Document Date: November 2, 2022

SUMMARY
A shooting incident in Milwaukee, WI resulted in the recovery of three
firearms. The incident type was a shooting. Two of the recovered weapons were
pistols manufactured by Glock; one was a rifle manufactured by Ruger.

DEALER AND PURCHASER
One recovered pistol was sold by Eagle Point Firearms. The weapon was purchased
by Marcus Webb in 2021. A second firearm traced back to a private reseller in
Gary, IN. The buyer of the rifle could not be conclusively identified.

NOTES
This record is connected to the broader trafficking pattern described in Case
No: ATF-2023-0457. Investigators flagged Eagle Point Firearms for repeated
appearances across multiple recoveries in Illinois and Wisconsin.
