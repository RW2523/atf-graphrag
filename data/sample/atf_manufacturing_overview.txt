ATF MANUFACTURING AND COMMERCE OVERVIEW
Report No: ATF-MFG-2023-08
Document Date: August 9, 2023

MANUFACTURERS
Glock and Smith & Wesson remain among the most frequently traced manufacturers
in urban trafficking cases. Ruger rifles appear in a smaller but notable share
of recoveries. Manufacturing volume for pistols continued to rise through 2023.

DISTRIBUTION
Licensed dealers such as Eagle Point Firearms distribute to retail buyers across
the Midwest. A recurring distribution route runs from Gary, IN into Chicago, IL
and Milwaukee, WI. This route overlaps with several trafficking investigations.

TRENDS
Across recovered firearms, the pistol is the dominant firearm type, and
trafficking is the most common incident type associated with multi-state
recoveries. Repeat dealers and repeat purchasers are the strongest indicators of
organized diversion.
